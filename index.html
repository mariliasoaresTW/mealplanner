Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs & Microgreens
A nourishing, vibrant breakfast that's as beautiful as it is delicious. Creamy avocado on crispy sourdough, crowned with silky poached eggs and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes.
480Calories
28gProtein
38gCarbs
24gFat
9gFiber

How to Make It

1

Prepare the Avocado

Halve the ripe avocado, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash with a fork until creamy but still slightly chunky. Season with sea salt, black pepper, and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Stir in a pinch of garlic powder.

3 mins
2

Toast the Bread

Place the sourdough slices in a toaster or on a skillet over medium heat. Toast until golden brown and crisp around the edges — this is your canvas, so get it right.

4 mins
3

Poach the Eggs

Bring a small pot of water to a gentle simmer. Add a splash of white vinegar. Create a gentle whirlpool with a spoon. Crack each egg into a small ramekin first, then carefully slip into the centre of the swirl. Cook 3–4 minutes for a runny yolk. Remove with a slotted spoon and rest on paper towel.

7 mins
4

Assemble & Serve

Spread the mashed avocado generously over the toast. Place a poached egg on each slice. Scatter microgreens, a pinch of red pepper flakes, and finish with a drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil and flaky sea salt. Serve immediately.

2 mins
